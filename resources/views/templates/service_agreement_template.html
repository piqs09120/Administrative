1. DEFINITIONS

1.4 "Hotel Policies"
means Hotel's written policies provided or made available to Service Provider, including brand standards, health & safety, data protection, and anti-bribery policies.
2. SCOPE; PERFORMANCE STANDARDS
2.1 Professional Performance
Service Provider shall perform the Services in a professional, workmanlike manner consistent with industry standards and the SLA.
2.2 Personnel Qualifications
Service Provider shall ensure personnel are suitably qualified, adequately supervised, and comply with Hotel Policies while on Hotel premises.
2.3 Onsite Services [Optional]
Service Provider personnel shall sign in at security, wear visible ID, and follow all access rules, including quiet hours and guest privacy.

7.1 Compliance

7.3 Background Checks [Optional]

8.1 Confidential Information

9.1 Data Processing

9.2 Data Security
Service Provider will implement industry‑standard administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect Guest Data against unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss, and shall notify Hotel without undue delay of any data incident.
9.3 PCI‑DSS [Optional—if handling card data]

10.2 Deliverables

13.1 Exclusions
EXCEPT FOR (A) A PARTY'S INDEMNIFICATION OBLIGATIONS, (B) BREACH OF CONFIDENTIALITY, (C) DATA PROTECTION BREACHES, OR (D) FRAUD, GROSS NEGLIGENCE, OR WILLFUL MISCONDUCT, NEITHER PARTY SHALL BE LIABLE FOR INDIRECT, INCIDENTAL, SPECIAL, CONSEQUENTIAL, OR PUNITIVE DAMAGES.
