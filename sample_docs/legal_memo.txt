SUBJECT: Review of Special Event Requirements

This memorandum outlines the legal requirements for hosting corporate events that may require additional security clearance and visitor coordination.

KEY LEGAL CONSIDERATIONS:
1. All visitors must be properly registered 48 hours in advance
2. Security clearance required for government officials
3. Liability insurance must be verified for events over 50 attendees
4. Local permits may be required for events with alcohol service

COMPLIANCE STATUS: REQUIRES LEGAL REVIEW

The event described in the facility reservation request triggers multiple compliance requirements that must be addressed before approval.

RECOMMENDATION: Legal department review required before final approval.
